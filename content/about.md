I'm Van Huynh, a software engineer who enjoys building things with **TypeScript**, **Python**, **Go**, and **Tailwind CSS**.

I believe in writing clean, maintainable code and sharing what I learn along the way. This blog is where I document my journey -- technical deep dives, project breakdowns, and lessons learned.
